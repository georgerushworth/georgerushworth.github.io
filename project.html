Introduction

The first signal of a disconnect is velocity. As the chart below shows, the "Magnificent 7" tech giants have completely decoupled from the broader market. Since January 2023, the Mag 7 index has surged over 200%, driving market returns largely on its own, while the equal-weighted S&P 500 has risen a modest 40%. This divergence forces a critical question: is this premium driven by superior economic fundamentals, or by narrative momentum?

Is Artifical Intelligence (AI) a bubble? The debate has largely settled into two camps. Those in Camp 1 roll their eyes and say it is all hype, while those in Camp 2 are convinced AI is the next industrial revolution in real time.
