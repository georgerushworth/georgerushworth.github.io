The Two Cycles of AI

Anatomy of a Nuanced Bubble

Aims

This project dissects the AI boom by separating the speculative Financial Cycle from the utility driven Technological Cycle. Using automated data, I aim to quantify how media buzz drives stock prices, contrast infrastructure spending against revenue realities, and use the 1998 Dot-com bubble to demonstrate why financial crashes often fund technological revolutions.
