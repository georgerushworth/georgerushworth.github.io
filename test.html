CC3: Debating

Task: Produce two charts that support or refute (or are related to in some way) a topic discussed at the Festival of Economics.

Position: Governments should prioritise growth-oriented tax cuts even when unfunded, as economic expansion will generate offsetting revenues. Truss's mini-budget will be used as a case-study.

Impact on Bank Rate

Refute #1: Unfunded tax cuts in Truss's 2022 mini-budget fueled inflation fears, prompting steeper rate hikes that stifled growth instead of boosting it.

Impact on Gilt Yield

Refute #2: Truss's 2022 mini-budget spiked gilt yields and crashed investor confidence, proving unfunded tax cuts can destabilise markets, not generate offsetting revenues.
